Week 5 - October 6, 2025

What I Have Worked On

This week, I worked on Project 1 with my teammate. Our project is called AirQuest, a terminal-based game that uses real-time air quality data from the World Air Quality Index (WAQI) API. The users can enter different cities and gain or lose health percentages depending on the air quality of the city.
We also used PyFiglet, Rich, and Colorama to add visual with ASCII art, color-coded messages, and poetic warnings to make it a bit more fun to play.
This project was really fun, and I learned a lot about the stylizing the terminal and making text-based games more engaging. The gamification part also made the experience a lot more interesting than simply printing data to the screen.

Challenges / Problems

Working in the terminal was more challenging than I expected as there are some things we can't do easily, like precise layout or GIFs. It required some experimentation to make the visuals look good. Overall, I learned a lot through this project and had fun creating the game.
